General questions

What is AMC?

It's simply an agreement between your company and a maintenance
services provider that they will provide regular maintenance to your company
under the terms of the contract.


What are the various mode of payment accepted here?

You can make payment via gpay, netbanking.


What are the benefits of having an AMC?

Regular maintenance
Cost-effective
Priority service
Reduced downtime


What services are included in an AMC?

Regular maintenance visits
Repair services
Emergency services
Replacement of parts
Technical support
Calibration and testing


Can an AMC be renewed after it expires?

YES, The AMC can be renewed after it has been expired


How long does an AMC last?

The AMC lasts for 1 year or 12 months


What happens if I don't renew my AMC?

If you don't renew your Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) before it expires, you will no longer be entitled to the benefits and services provided under the contract. This means that any repairs or maintenance required will be at your own cost, and you will no longer have access to priority service or faster response times.
